ABOUT NOTA INC.

At Nota we help people create, think, and discuss new ideas together. Our goal is to broaden the horizon of web sharing services every day. We want to build services that take less effort to make something great in order to help people do more productive, creative things. May every small click help you realize your big dreams.

Communication builds relationships, businesses, and the world. We're working to make communication on the web more natural, fluid, and direct from offices in the United States and Japan.
